2. Style: High Fantasy, Final Fantasy inspired, JRPG like.
3. Plot Hook: A villain is trying to drop a flying city on top of the Imperial Capitol.
4. Climax: The villain corrupts de Air Crystal and starts a "colony drop".
5. Key NPCs: A female mage that was investigating the villain and has the uncorrupting force to control the Air Crystal again. A member of the Imperial Guard of the Flying City that begs the group of heroes to repel the enemy creatures that were summoned in order to prevent them from getting to the Crystal and fixing it. A female spy from the enemy Kingdom that is taking advantage of the chaos to steal the Crystal and take it, offering the heroes something very valuable if they help her (she also discloses the Imperial Kingdom's massacre of an ancient people which the villain is part of and is trying to avenge).
6. Social Encounter: With the female spy.
7. Combat scenarios: Against the summoned creatures. Against the main villain. Against the spy (if they choose to do so).
